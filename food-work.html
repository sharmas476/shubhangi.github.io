The PLEDGE on Food Waste, a leading certification and benchmarking system, wanted to redesign their website for a more modern and engaging look. The focus was on improving usability, showcasing their mission, and creating a seamless user experience to amplify their global impact.

The PLEDGE stands as a global certification solution, integrating best practices to reduce waste from procurement to disposal. At their core, they prioritize sustainability, empowerment, collaboration, innovation, community, and transparency as the cornerstones of approach.
